I am Alexis

My career started in Marseille at the age of 15 with my mum part of my first teachers.
At the age of 19, i had graduated twice from the hairdressing school .

Two years later, I carried on my career as a freelancer and then wanted to travel the world at the same time and chase my dreams.
At the age of 24, I got a distinction for my third diploma in England which is recognised worldwide.

More then a perfectionist and a passionate hairdresser who specialises both in ladies and gents hair, as well as barbering, Iâ€™m excited about new experiences, motivated by the challenges.

I moved to London without speaking English but soon i managed to overcome the language barrier and i managed to take my career a step further by joining the TONI&GUY Flagship salon in Shoreditch. I was promoted to an assistant manager in 18 months.

I keep experiences diverse by doing external events as well as working backstage alongside the artistic team of TONI&GUY for London fashion week.

Today I count 10 years of experience in the hairdressing industry.
