Hi I am Alexis

Originally from the south of France, I realised very early that I had a strong passion for hairdressing & barbering. My career started in Marseille at the age of 15 with my mum being one of my first teachers.
At the age of 19, I had graduated twice from the hairdressing school. I then carried on my career as self employed for a few years.

Inspired by new experiences, motivated by the challenges, I decided to move to London at the age of 24 with no English skills, purely focused on my passion for hairdressing and self-development.

I took my career a step further by joining the TONI&GUY Flagship salon in Shoreditch where I had to start as a apprentice again but soon I managed to overcome the language barrier and I got a distinction for my third diploma at the prestigious TONI&GUY LONDON ACADEMY which is recognised worldwide.
Then I worked my way up and became the ASSISTANT MANAGER / STYLE DIRECTOR / STAFF TRAINER in 18 months.

London gave me the opportunity to explore my boundaries and work in diverse environments as well as interact with people from all sorts of backgrounds.

I keep experiences diverse by doing external events as well as working backstage alongside the International Artistic Team of TONI&GUY for various LONDON FASHION WEEK.

I am proud to have worked alongside brands like CHANEL, HUGO BOSS, LOUBOUTIN or magazine like BRITISH GQ and GQ CHINA.

All those formative years allowed me to further hone my craft .

Today I count 11 years of experience as a hairdresser and I am now self employed again but this time, based in LONDON.
