The real Alexis

Alex has been in the hairdressing industry since 2007 in a range of Salons and as a freelancer as well. He moved from Marseille to London 2017 to work at a flagship TONI&GUY salon, where he was promoted to assistant manager in 18 months. He keeps his experience diverse by doing external events as well.

He is a perfectionist and a passionate hairdresser who specialises in both ladies and gents hair, as well as barbering.

“My education and my inspiration knows no limits. I am always striving to do better and offer the best for my clients!”

Languages Spoken: English, French
